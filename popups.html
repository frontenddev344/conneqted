One Cable, Unlimited Potential – Power Over Ethernet (PoE)

Most people think of an Ethernet cable as just a way to deliver internet—but with Power over Ethernet (PoE), it does much more. PoE technology allows a single Ethernet cable to provide both data and low-voltage power, eliminating the need for separate electrical wiring.

That means devices like access points, cameras, and smart sensors can be powered and connected through one reliable cable—making your network cleaner, more efficient, and far more resilient. In the modern connected home, PoE is the backbone of smart, secure infrastructure.

  • Smarter & Safer Power

    Delivers both electricity and internet through a single cable—cutting costs, reducing clutter, and eliminating the need for extra wiring or outlets.

  • One Cable, Double the Function

    Powers and connects security cameras, smart switches, access points, and more—no batteries, no frequent replacements.

  • Cleaner Aesthetics, Fewer Failure Points

    No bulky adapters or wall plugs; just a clean, centralized system with fewer things that can go wrong.

  • Zero Maintenance, Maximum Reliability

    PoE networks are built to last, with less wear and tear and virtually no ongoing maintenance.

  • Effortless Smart Home Integration

    Simplifies automation for lighting, security, and devices—without the hassle of complex installations or wiring upgrades.
