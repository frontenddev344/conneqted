About Conneqted

No two homes are the same, and no system should be, either. Every Conneqted design is fully customized to match the layout, needs, and goals of the space.

Conneqted was founded by Deren Goldin and Oran Aviv with a shared goal: to create smart, reliable, low-voltage systems. With backgrounds in engineering, construction, networking, and technology, they built Conneqted to deliver seamless integration across security, automation, and connectivity.

What sets Conneqted apart is a design-driven approach built around each property’s unique layout, lifestyle, and needs. Every system is custom-built—never off-the-shelf—ensuring that components work together seamlessly and perform reliably from day one.

Our Process

Preconstruction Consultation

The consultation begins with a strategic walkthrough of the property’s layout and project goals. During this session, a high-level system is developed to determine the ideal placement for devices, wiring, access points, and system zones.

As part of this process, two system design paths are created:

  • Recommended – a best-in-class solution designed for full coverage, premium performance, and long-term scalability.
  • Essential – a streamlined system that includes all necessary core functionality in a more cost-conscious format.
1

Network Planning

A network strategy is developed based on the home’s layout, usage needs, and device demands. Placement of outlets, network switches, access points, and hard-wired components is planned to ensure full coverage, minimize latency, and support future scalability.

2

Scope Review Call

The scope review provides a clear overview of all proposed systems, equipment, and coverage areas. This includes security, audio, networking, and access controls. The review ensures alignment with your goals and confirms that the proposed system meets performance expectations.
