Networking Resilience

Most homes use mesh Wi-Fi systems that rely on multiple plug-in nodes scattered throughout the house.

The problem? If even one of those nodes loses power or fails, your entire network can be compromised. And to keep the network online during a power outage, you’d need backup power at every single outlet where a node is plugged in.

With our PoE (Power over Ethernet) network, resilience is centralized. You only need to back up your modem, main router, and switch. That means even if the rest of your home loses power, your entire network can keep running—streaming, security, automation, and more—all from a single, protected hub.
